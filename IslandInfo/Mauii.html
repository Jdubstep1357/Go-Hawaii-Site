Mauii

The 'Modern' Island

Swim with dolphins!

Swimming with dolphins is really fun. It requires a lot of work and a lot of play a well. Bottlenecks, Spinner, among others really are great to swim with. Bring your entire family to make the entire thing super fun!

Jet Skii

Surf the waves bro. See how far and how high you can go with the waves. With this technology, one can be able to ride for hours one end with the most fun time. Definately consider joining. It's wavetastic!

Hike in the bamboo forest

Take a long walk out in nature. Bamboo roots are sometimes over centuries old. Don't worry about other things going amiss. Just chill out and enjoy one's walk outside.

Visit the plantations

Hawaii is founded on good ol American Capitalism. Mauii is no exception. Learn the history of how the entire island is a huge hub of food and one may be able to truly understand the implications of what is to come.

Go bird watching

Bird watching is a great thing for people to do. It's calming, relaxing and also allows others to relax and enjoy the nature around themselves. Hawaii is known for a lot of endangered and exotic plant as well as animal life. Who knows? Maybe one day you could get lucky.

Climb the dragon's teeth

Do you have the teeth to tackle this dangerous cavern? Dragon's teeth is renounded for its ability to captivate tourists with its beautiful views and scenic routes.
