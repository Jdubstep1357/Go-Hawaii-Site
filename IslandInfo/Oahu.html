Oahu

The 'Outdoor' Island

Survive Sharks

Swimming with dolphins is really fun. It requires a lot of work and a lot of play a well. Bottlenecks, Spinner, among others really are great to swim with. Bring your entire family to make the entire thing super fun!

Bus Tour

Bus rides sure are fun! Especially when there are mountains right beneath you! Tons of wildlife is also important, as many viewers see a lot of the local wildlife. Get signed up for GoHawaii's bus ride today!

Helicopter Rides

See the island, from the air! Many viewers have had nothing but positive things from the helicopter rides that people take. Riding in helicopters will surely make things much more scenic and beautiful.

Paragliding

Test the skies with paragliding. Paragliding will allow many people to not only have fun, but to also see the entire island. Paragliding is surely a test of fun and effort. Fly the skies and see how far you can go with paragliding. Paragliding sure is a fun time.

Zip Line

Zip! That's the sound of hovering over the ground when going below the mountain. I believe every person should zip line sometime in their life. You not only explore the island but also have a lot of fun! Sign up and get involved today!

Kareoke

A trip to Hawaii would not be complete without a kareoke bar. Test your dance skills as well as your dance skills. Kareoke is only meant for the bold and the brave, especially after a hard day's work at whatever journey you and your family has gone through.
