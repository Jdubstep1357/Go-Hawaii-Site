Kauai

The 'Turtle' Island

Camp and hike in the canyons

Who likes marshmellow and warm fires? Kauai is known for its beautiful and lush camping grounds. It is truly a spectable to behold when one can see stars clearly at night. One cannot see stars from other places.

Hike and swim in waterfalls

Hawaii has never been more beautiful than today. Being able to go hiking and swimming is purely a treat for people. Taking photos by the waterfall is very beautiful thing. One should always consider hiking in this tropical, sublime climate. Just make sure to bring insect repellant, as the hike up is a long one, but worth the effort.

We're All in the same Boat

Learning how to paddle and survive is something that is very important for many travelors. Especially when there is a lot of water beneath you. Truly, one should be inspired by all of the water around them. It is not unheard of dolphins or whales coming by the boats.

Set Sail!

Sign up today for sailing the island seas. Sailing from Kaui's locaiton can allow people to travel to different islands, which is something that is really interesting to do. It is something to take off the bucket list. Especially when the sun begins to set over the crevise below

Go Offroad!

Going accross other areas is truly something that one should be aware of, especially with everything else going on. Wildlife, forests, beaches and even caynons are a few of the obstacles that one can take when going off the road. Going offroad is very fun and very cool. Every college-aged adult should be apart of it.

Don't be a hare

Swimming with turtles are only a few fun things that people can get involved with when doing certain activities. I, myself, have seen more than my share of wild animals in the water and reccomend it to you.
